## Installation
Extract ``lan_of_doom_disable_round_timer.tar.gz`` to your server's ``css/cstrike``
directory.

## Console Variables
``sm_lanofdoom_round_timer_disabled`` If ``1``, the round timer is disabled.
``1`` by default.
